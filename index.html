The following map represents the forestry density in each county in Tennessee. The darker shaded counties show the land that have the highest proportion of forest area. I chose this topic because I used to live in Tennessee and I remember driving through a few counties and seeing heavy forest areas and thought it'd be interesting to see the distribution of forestry in the state.
